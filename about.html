Inspiration

MCEF was inspired by the keen Observation that was taken by the founder of MCEF on this communityâ€™s (Matale) youths, women and children. Over the years, the key productive aspects of skills, knowledge, attitude and values have been inadequate among the categories of people (above) in the rural communities of Kyotera and Rakai districts especially Matale village. Therefore an initiative to let people participate in empowerment drive evolved because of the less privileged nature of the community members. However through trainings, provision of education and sensitization programs, the rural communities can be revived. Once the initiatives are thoroughly implemented, the communities will have valuable and productive members that lead to community growth and wellbeing.

History

Matale community empowerment foundation (MCEF) is a Ugandan rural development and empowerment NGO founded in 2023 to empower rural communities through dynamic, diverse but simple programs that directly involve and benefit rural community members, especially children, women and the youth regardless of their religion, tribe, sex or physical appearance.

Location

We are currently operating in rural communities of Kyotera and Rakia districts in the central region of Uganda.
