WHAT WE DO ?

Overview

MCEF is aiming to develop a national and international network of experienced professionals that serve as support team and a wider network of individuals and groups that significantly mentor children.

MCEF’s innovative programs are run by experienced, professional, local and national staff and MCEF is well placed to partner with funding bodies and other service providers.

We intend to break the chain of poverty and in due course empower rural communities to sustain their own wellbeing and development. This is directed to be achieved through the direct involvement of the community leadership that drive the community members to work together to achieve the desired change through the support of MCEF.

About us

Matale community empowerment foundation (MCEF) is a Ugandan rural development and empowerment NGO founded in 2023 to empower rural communities through dynamic, diverse but simple programs that directly involve and benefit rural community members, especially children, women and the youth regardless of their religion, tribe, sex or physical appearance.

We are currently operating in rural communities of Kyotera and Rakia districts in the central region of Uganda.
