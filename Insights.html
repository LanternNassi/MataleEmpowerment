WHAT WE DO.

MCEF has since its founding conducted awareness campaigns and community dialogues, on proper healthcare, benefits of education, it has afforded school scholarships to children from vulnerable homes, has raised awareness on the dangers of child marriages and broached a discussion on gender inequalities and how the communities can do better to promote gender equality. MCEF has also conducted capacity building drives that have trained the beneficiaries on practical skills, financial book keeping skills and marketing. MCEF intends to continue serving the communities of Kyotera and Rakai districts to foster mindset change and using the community-based approach and beneficiary- centered approach to customize initiatives basing on the needs of the target communities. Advocacy will take centre stage in the work of MCEF and this is aimed at giving the communities a sense of ownership and empower them to demand reform and accountability from the duty-bearers in their respective communities.

About us

Matale community empowerment foundation (MCEF) is a Ugandan rural development and empowerment NGO founded in 2023 to empower rural communities through dynamic, diverse but simple programs that directly involve and benefit rural community members, especially children, women and the youth regardless of their religion, tribe, sex or physical appearance.

We are currently operating in rural communities of Kyotera and Rakia districts in the central region of Uganda.
